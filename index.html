Why Computer Science?

At the young age of three years, I began exploring technology through learning apps, and it quickly became a part of my everyday life. As I grew older, I noticed how essential technology became: not just for entertainment or education, but for how people live, work, and connect. I knew early on I wanted a future in the tech-world -- I just did not know where I would fit in.
It wasn't until I started exploring programming and computer science that everything finally clicked. The problem-solving, logic, and creativity involved in coding, as well as the captivating complexity of technology, made it clear that this is the path I want to follow. Through classes, projects, and hands-on experience, I’ve found a deep passion for computer science -- and I’m excited to keep learning and building in this ever-evolving field.
